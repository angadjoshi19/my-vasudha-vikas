MY वसुधा विकास

(विधायक कार्य संघ, अकोला)

स्वच्छ, सुंदर, प्लॅस्टिक मुक्त महाराष्ट्राचा ध्यास

प्रयत्न आमचे, सहकार्य तुमचे

A leading grassroots environment protection movement dedicating over 15 years to single-use plastic eradication, regional afforestation development, and upcycled resource management circles.

Our Primary Motive

To reverse escalating public environmental degradation, counteract corporate single-use convenience mentalities, and intercept toxic plastic waste across localized municipal pathways to protect planetary well-being.

Our Mission Statement

To completely eliminate non-biodegradable packaging variants from household functions, public dining conventions, and large festive arrays, swapping them out with scalable circular reusable alternatives.

Resource Contribution Dashboard

Welcome to our dedicated Donation Dashboard. Every contribution is securely managed and strictly routed into our resource lifecycles:
• Fabric Waste: Upcycled by our volunteers into free, durable cloth carrier bags to replace single-use plastic bags.
• Milk Packets: Down-cut and upcycled into protective nursery pots to grow and protect young afforestation saplings.
• Financial Dropbox Support: 100% of funds directly finance the acquisition of commercial automated maintenance systems and fuel local resource transport networks crossways to 120 villages in Maharashtra.
